About ExamDino

A massive study framework, not just a landing page.

ExamDino is designed to help IGCSE students find their course, collect notes, practice with past-paper style drills, and assemble a realistic revision plan in one deployable app.

How it works

Local-first, with AI as a fallback layer

The app first uses deterministic processing: keyword extraction, extractive summaries, flashcards, and quiz generation. That keeps it reliable on Render without needing a model key.

If you want to connect a hosted AI service later, the upload and notes pipeline already has a clean slot for it. For now, the framework is fully usable with local heuristics.
